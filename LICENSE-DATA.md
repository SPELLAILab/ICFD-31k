# ICFD-31k Data Use Terms

ICFD-31k is released under a **restricted, defensive-use license** to reduce the dual-use risk inherent in fraud-conversation data. The dataset, examples, and annotation files are made available for defensive research and education only, under the terms below.

## Permitted Uses

- Academic research on fraud detection and prevention.
- Development and evaluation of defensive AI systems.
- Educational use in cybersecurity, NLP, AI safety, and responsible AI courses.
- Benchmarking models for conversational fraud detection.

## Prohibited Uses

- Generating scam scripts or social-engineering material.
- Training, fine-tuning, or evaluating systems intended to commit fraud.
- Building offensive tools that automate deception, impersonation, or credential theft.
- Commercial deployment without explicit written permission from the authors.

## Privacy

All names, phone numbers, account details, and other personally identifiable information are synthetic. The dataset does not contain real call recordings or real victim data.

## Citation

Use of the dataset requires citation of the ICFD-31k paper or repository.
