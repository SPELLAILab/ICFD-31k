[SCENARIO 1]
description: Fake cryptocurrency exchange platform promising 50% monthly returns through "AI-powered trading algorithms". Scammer poses as investment advisor pressuring victim to transfer Bitcoin or Ethereum to unverified wallet addresses.
case_type: Clear_Fraud
policy:
Rule A: Legitimate crypto platforms never guarantee fixed returns or promise specific profit percentages
Rule B: Real exchanges require KYC verification and are registered with financial regulators
Rule C: Investment advisors must be licensed and cannot pressure immediate fund transfers
Rule D: No legitimate platform requests direct wallet-to-wallet transfers bypassing exchange systems
fraud_indicators: ["guaranteed returns", "AI trading bot", "limited time offer", "direct wallet transfer", "unregistered platform"]
legitimate_indicators: ["regulatory compliance", "no return guarantees", "proper KYC", "licensed advisors"]
