[SCENARIO 1]
description: Fake disaster relief charity scam following major natural disaster (earthquake, hurricane, wildfire). Scammer creates website mimicking real charity, uses emotional images, and requests donations via wire transfer or gift cards with no tax receipts.
case_type: Clear_Fraud
policy:
Rule A: Legitimate charities are registered with IRS and state charity regulators
Rule B: Real disaster relief organizations provide tax-deductible receipts and EIN numbers
Rule C: Authentic charities accept credit cards and checks, rarely request gift cards or crypto
Rule D: Legitimate organizations have verifiable track records and annual financial reports
fraud_indicators: ["unregistered charity", "no EIN number", "gift card donations", "no tax receipts", "newly created website"]
legitimate_indicators: ["IRS registration", "tax receipts", "verified charity status", "published financials", "charity navigator rating"]
