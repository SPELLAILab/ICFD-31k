[SCENARIO 1]
description: IRS impersonation scam with aggressive threats. Scammer claims victim owes back taxes and must pay immediately via gift cards or wire transfer to avoid arrest warrant, wage garnishment, or deportation. Uses spoofed IRS phone numbers.
case_type: Clear_Fraud
policy:
Rule A: IRS never initiates contact about taxes owed via phone call without prior mail notice
Rule B: Real IRS never demands immediate payment via gift cards, wire transfer, or cryptocurrency
Rule C: Authentic IRS communications don't threaten arrest or deportation for tax debt
Rule D: Legitimate tax matters are resolved through proper IRS channels and payment plans
fraud_indicators: ["immediate payment demand", "gift card payment", "arrest threats", "deportation threats", "no prior mail contact"]
legitimate_indicators: ["mail correspondence first", "official payment methods", "no threats", "proper IRS channels", "payment plan options"]
