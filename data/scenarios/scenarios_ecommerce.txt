[SCENARIO 1]
description: A seller lists a [FURNITURE_ITEM] on [MARKETPLACE_PLATFORM]. A "buyer" calls, agrees to the price instantly without negotiation, and insists on paying the full amount immediately via [UPI_APP] to "book" the item. They then send a QR code and urgently instruct the seller to scan it and enter their PIN to receive the money.
case_type: Clear Fraud
platform_type: marketplace
context: C2C marketplace transaction with reverse payment scam
fraud_technique: Reverse UPI QR code scam
policy:
Rule A: Receiving money via UPI never requires the recipient to scan a QR code or enter their PIN.
Rule B: Buyers who rush payment without inspecting an item are a high-risk red flag.
